Slam Tilt

The pinball game.

Description:

    Slam Tilt, an arcade game (pinball simulator) developed by Liquid Dezign HB, and released in 1996 by 21st Century Entertainment for Amiga computers. In 1997, a version for PC personal computers was created.

    The game is the last in a series of Amiga pinball titles released by the publisher, including Pinball Fantasies and Pinball Dreams.

    Just like the previous games, it featured four themed tables:
  • Mean Machines
  • The Pirate
  • Ace of Space
  • Night of the Demon

    • Each table features an animated LCD panel at the top of the screen upon which scores and animations are displayed.

    The game also offers additional challenges through 'video modes' in which the player must use the LCD panel and table to complete further objectives.
